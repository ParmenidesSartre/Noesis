# Feature 9: Analytics & Reporting

**Document Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** 2026-01-26

## 9.1 Enrollment Trends

### 9.1.1 Enrollment Analytics

**Enrollment Metrics:**
- Total active students (current count)
- New enrollments (this month/term/year)
- Re-enrollments (returning students)
- Withdrawals (students who left)
- Net enrollment change (new - withdrawals)
- Enrollment capacity utilization (% of total capacity)
- Enrollment by:
  - Branch
  - Subject/course
  - Grade level
  - Class type (group/private)

**Enrollment Trends:**
- Line graph showing enrollment over time (monthly, quarterly, annually)
- Year-over-year comparison
- Month-over-month comparison
- Seasonal patterns (peak enrollment months, slow months)
- Enrollment forecast (predictive, based on historical data)

**Enrollment Conversion:**
- Inquiry to enrollment conversion rate
- Trial class to enrollment conversion rate
- Lead source effectiveness (which marketing channels bring most enrollments)
- Enrollment funnel visualization

**Enrollment Segmentation:**
- New students vs returning students
- Enrollment by age group
- Enrollment by demographics (if tracked)
- Enrollment by referral source

### 9.1.2 Enrollment Reports

**Enrollment Summary Report:**
- Total enrollment snapshot
- Breakdown by branch, grade, subject
- Comparison to targets (if set)
- Export to Excel/PDF

**Detailed Enrollment Report:**
- List of all enrolled students
- Enrollment date
- Subjects enrolled
- Fee structure
- Filter and sort options
- Export for analysis

**Enrollment Projections:**
- Based on trends, project future enrollment
- Enrollment capacity planning
- Identify growth opportunities or concerns

## 9.2 Revenue Reports

### 9.2.1 Financial Metrics

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Total revenue (this month/term/year)
- Revenue by payment status:
  - Collected revenue (paid invoices)
  - Outstanding revenue (unpaid/overdue invoices)
  - Projected revenue (future invoices)
- Revenue by:
  - Branch
  - Subject/course
  - Grade level
  - Payment method
  - Student cohort

**Revenue Trends:**
- Revenue over time (daily, weekly, monthly, yearly)
- Revenue growth rate
- Year-over-year comparison
- Month-over-month comparison
- Revenue forecast

**Revenue Per Student:**
- Average revenue per student
- Lifetime value of student
- Revenue by student segment (grade, subject, etc.)

**Fee Collection Rate:**
- % of invoices paid on time
- % of invoices overdue
- Average days to payment
- Bad debt rate (uncollectible invoices written off)

### 9.2.2 Revenue Reports

**Monthly Revenue Report:**
- Total revenue for the month
- Breakdown by source (tuition fees, material fees, etc.)
- Comparison to budget/target
- Outstanding invoices
- Cash flow summary

**Revenue by Subject:**
- Which subjects generate most revenue
- Which subjects are underperforming
- Profitability per subject

**Revenue by Branch:**
- Branch-wise revenue comparison
- Highest and lowest performing branches
- Branch contribution to total revenue

**Payment Method Report:**
- Revenue by payment method (cash, card, bank transfer, etc.)
- Payment processing fees
- Preferred payment methods

**Financial Dashboard:**
- Real-time financial overview
- Key metrics (total revenue, outstanding, collected)
- Visual charts (pie chart, bar graph, line trend)
- Filterable by date range, branch, etc.

### 9.2.3 Expense and Profit Reports

**Expense Tracking:**
- Operational expenses:
  - Teacher salaries/payments
  - Rent and utilities
  - Materials and supplies
  - Marketing expenses
  - Administrative costs
  - Technology costs
- Expense by category and branch

**Profit Analysis:**
- Revenue minus expenses = Profit
- Profit margin (%)
- Profit by branch
- Profit by subject/course
- Break-even analysis

**Budget vs Actual:**
- Compare actual revenue and expenses to budget
- Variance analysis
- Identify over/under spending

## 9.3 Attendance Statistics

### 9.3.1 Attendance Metrics

**Overall Attendance:**
- System-wide attendance rate (%)
- Attendance by:
  - Branch
  - Grade level
  - Subject
  - Class
  - Day of week
  - Time of day

**Attendance Trends:**
- Attendance rate over time (daily, weekly, monthly)
- Seasonal attendance patterns
- Identify low attendance periods (e.g., exam weeks, holidays)

**Student Attendance:**
- Individual student attendance %
- Students with perfect attendance
- Students with low attendance (<X%)
- Chronic absenteeism (frequent absences)

**Class Attendance:**
- Class-wise attendance average
- Best attended classes
- Poorly attended classes (investigate reasons)

### 9.3.2 Attendance Reports

**Attendance Summary Report:**
- Overview of attendance across all classes
- Total sessions, total attended, total absent
- Attendance percentage
- Filterable by date range, branch, grade, etc.

**Student Attendance Report:**
- Individual student's full attendance record
- All classes, all dates
- Absence reasons
- Late arrivals
- Export to PDF for parents/records

**Class Attendance Report:**
- Session-by-session attendance for a class
- Which students were present/absent each session
- Attendance trends for the class

**Attendance Analytics:**
- Correlation between attendance and performance
- Impact of absences on grades
- Students at risk due to low attendance

## 9.4 Teacher Performance Metrics

### 9.4.1 Performance Indicators

**Teaching Load:**
- Number of classes taught
- Number of students taught
- Total teaching hours per week/month
- Workload distribution (balanced or overloaded)

**Student Performance:**
- Average grades of teacher's students
- Pass rate (% of students passing)
- Improvement rate (avg improvement of students over term)
- Student satisfaction (from surveys/feedback)

**Retention Rate:**
- % of teacher's students who re-enroll next term
- Student withdrawal rate from teacher's classes
- Comparison to centre average

**Class Attendance:**
- Average attendance in teacher's classes
- Teacher's own attendance/punctuality

**Administrative Metrics:**
- Timeliness of attendance submission
- Timeliness of grade submission
- Response time to parent/student messages
- Participation in meetings and training

### 9.4.2 Teacher Performance Reports

**Individual Teacher Report:**
- Summary of teacher's performance across all metrics
- Strengths and areas for improvement
- Comparison to centre benchmarks
- Historical performance trend

**Teacher Comparison Report:**
- Compare all teachers side-by-side
- Ranking (if applicable)
- Identify top performers
- Identify teachers needing support

**Teacher Feedback Summary:**
- Aggregate student and parent feedback
- Common themes (positive and negative)
- Actionable insights

**Teacher Development:**
- Track professional development activities
- Certifications earned
- Training attended
- Skills developed

### 9.4.3 Performance Reviews

**Automated Performance Dashboard:**
- Teacher views own performance metrics
- Real-time updates
- Goal tracking (if goals set)

**Performance Review Process:**
- Periodic reviews (e.g., twice a year)
- Admin reviews teacher performance report
- Schedule performance review meeting
- Document review discussion
- Set goals for improvement
- Store review records

**Academic Performance:**
- Current grades (all subjects)

- Comparison to class average
- Comparison to own past performance
- Strengths and weaknesses by subject/topic

**Attendance & Engagement:**
- Attendance rate
- Participation score

**Predictive Analytics:**

**Progress Tracking:**
- Progress towards learning goals
- Skills mastery tracking
- Achievement badges/milestones

### 9.5.2 Class Performance Analytics

**Class Averages:**
- Average grade per subject
- Grade distribution (how many A's, B's, C's, etc.)

- Top performers

**Class Trends:**
- Performance over time
- Improvement or decline

### 9.5.3 Cohort Analysis

**Comparative Analysis:**

- Learn from past cohorts

## 9.6 Class Utilization Reports

### 9.6.1 Class Capacity Utilization

**Capacity Metrics:**
- Total class capacity (all classes combined)
- Current enrollment
- Capacity utilization rate (% of capacity filled)
- By branch, subject, grade, time slot

**Class Fill Rates:**
- Which classes are full (100% capacity)
- Which classes are underutilized (<50% capacity)
- Average class size
- Optimal vs actual class size

**Waitlist Analysis:**
- Number of students on waitlists
- Demand exceeding capacity (indicates need for more classes)
- By subject and time slot

### 9.6.2 Utilization Reports

**Class Utilization Summary:**
- Overview of all classes
- Enrollment vs capacity for each class
- Identify opportunities:
  - Classes to expand (high demand)
  - Classes to merge (low enrollment)
  - New class slots needed

**Time Slot Utilization:**
- Which days/times are most popular
- Which days/times are underutilized
- Optimize schedule based on demand

**Teacher Utilization:**
- How much of teacher's available time is utilized
- Identify underutilized teachers (can teach more classes)
- Identify overutilized teachers (at risk of burnout)

**Room Utilization:**
- % of time each classroom is in use
- Identify unused capacity (can add more classes)
- Optimize room assignments

### 9.6.3 Optimization Recommendations

**Data-Driven Suggestions:**
- System suggests:
  - Adding classes in high-demand time slots
  - Canceling or merging low-enrollment classes
  - Reassigning teachers to balance workload
  - Opening new branches/locations (based on demand)

**Scenario Planning:**
- What-if analysis (e.g., what if we add 2 more classes?)
- Capacity planning for next term

## 9.7 Financial Dashboards

### 9.7.1 Executive Dashboard

**Key Metrics:**
- Total revenue (MTD, YTD)
- Total expenses
- Net profit
- Profit margin
- Cash flow
- Outstanding receivables
- Student enrollment (current)
- Enrollment trend (vs last month/year)
- Retention rate

**Visual Widgets:**
- Revenue trend line chart
- Expense breakdown pie chart
- Enrollment funnel
- KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) with targets

**Real-Time Updates:**
- Dashboard updates in real-time or refresh on demand
- Drill-down capability (click on metric to see details)

### 9.7.2 Branch Financial Dashboard

**Branch Performance:**
- Revenue by branch
- Expenses by branch
- Profit by branch
- Enrollment by branch
- Comparison across branches

**Branch Scorecard:**
- Each branch's performance vs targets
- Ranking (top to bottom performing branches)
- Identify star performers and underperformers

### 9.7.3 Custom Dashboards

**Dashboard Builder:**
- Admin can create custom dashboards
- Select metrics to display
- Choose visualization type (chart, table, KPI card)
- Save and share dashboards

**Role-Based Dashboards:**
- Different dashboards for different roles:
  - Super Admin: Executive dashboard
  - Branch Admin: Branch dashboard
  - Teacher: Teaching dashboard (classes, students, performance)
  - Parent: Student progress dashboard

## 9.8 Custom Report Builder

### 9.8.1 Report Builder Interface

**Report Creation:**
- Select report type (student, class, financial, attendance, etc.)
- Select data fields to include (checkboxes)
- Apply filters:
  - Date range
  - Branch
  - Grade level
  - Subject
  - Teacher
  - Student
  - Any other relevant filters
- Choose grouping (group by subject, by month, etc.)
- Choose sorting (sort by name, by score, by date, etc.)

**Report Formats:**
- Table view (rows and columns)
- Summary view (aggregated totals)
- Chart view (bar chart, pie chart, line chart)
- Pivot table

**Report Customization:**
- Rename column headers
- Reorder columns
- Apply conditional formatting (e.g., highlight scores < 50 in red)
- Add calculated fields (e.g., Total = FieldA + FieldB)

### 9.8.2 Report Scheduling

**Scheduled Reports:**
- Schedule reports to run automatically:
  - Daily
  - Weekly
  - Monthly
  - Quarterly
  - Custom schedule
- Email report to recipients (admin, teachers, parents)
- Save report to repository

**Report Subscriptions:**
- Users can subscribe to reports
- Receive email when report is generated
- Access reports in their dashboard

### 9.8.3 Report Export and Sharing

**Export Formats:**
- Excel (XLSX)
- CSV
- PDF
- Google Sheets
- Print

**Sharing:**
- Share report link (view-only)
- Email report to specific users
- Download and distribute manually

**Report Templates:**
- Save custom reports as templates
- Reuse templates (just change filters/date range)
- Share templates with other admins
